Site Name: Monte Rio Route

The reason I chose this name it is beacuse there is a route of beaches that I know and they are like 5 beaches in the same route. I can do a website because I have a lot of photos of those beaches and I can simulate that I am selling trips to that place. The objective of the site is to sell trips for a route of beaches.

Site Purpose

The site has the objective to promote the tourism of the region and sell trips to a route of beaches. It will allow user to schedule trips with us, it will have an album of beaches to promote the tourism of the region, and will offer more information to the user.

Scenarios
